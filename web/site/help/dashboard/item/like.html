How to like an item?

  1. From home page or search results, you can just click on the image to go to item details page
  2. When on item details page, you will see a toolbar with icons and action links above the item image.
  3. click on "Like" link
  4. You can view your likes from your public page or your account

Just click on your user name to go to your public page.

To view your account, first Sign in. Then click on your name in site toolbar and select Account.
