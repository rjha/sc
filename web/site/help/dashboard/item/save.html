How to save an item to view later?

  1. From home page or search results, you can just click on the image to go to item details page
  2. When on item details page, you will see a toolbar with icons and action links below the item title and just above the item image.
  3. click on "Save" inside the toolbar
  4. You can save this item to an existing list or create a new list
  5. You can view your lists from your public page or your account

Just click on your user name to go to your public page.

To view all your lists from your account page, first Sign in using your email and password. Click on lists link in menu to view all your lists.
