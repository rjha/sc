How to save an item?

  1. First, Go to an item details page.
  2. There you can see a toolbar just after the title and above the main image.
  3. click on "save"
  4. Now the item should be added to your saved items

You can access your account by clicking on your name in top toolbar. From your account, click on saved items link to view the items you have saved.
