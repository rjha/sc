How to find interesting people to follow?

  1. From home page or search results, you can just click on the image to go to item details page
  2. When on item details page, you will see the name of user who uploaded that item
  3. Just click on follow button under the user name

How to stop following a user?

  1. After Sign in, go to your accounts page
  2. Click on More link in menus
  3. Select followings
  4. Just clik on unfollow button
